FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a Zupi account to buy tickets?
No account needed at all. Just click "Buy Tickets", complete your payment, and your ticket is sent to your email. Simple as that.
How will I receive my ticket?
Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive a digital ticket (QR code) to your email address. Show it at the door — no printing needed.
Can I get a refund?
Tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred. If the event is cancelled or postponed by Zupi, full refunds will be issued automatically.
Is there a minimum age requirement?
You must be 18+ to attend ZupiFest. Valid ID will be checked at the door.
When will the full lineup be announced?
Follow Zupi on socials and check back here — full lineup drops soon. Early ticket holders get notified first.
